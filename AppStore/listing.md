## Description
Take control of your finances with Ledger Lite — a clean, fast, and completely private expense tracker that lives entirely on your device.

Log what you spend in seconds. Ledger Lite lets you record expenses with an amount, category, currency, and note — all without an account, subscription, or internet connection. Your data never leaves your phone.

**What's inside:**
• Today view — see your daily total at a glance with a spending velocity badge and streak counter
• History — browse any past day with swipe navigation, search across all expenses, and see your monthly running total
• Subscriptions — track recurring bills with a billing calendar view that shows upcoming charges at a glance
• Insights — interactive donut chart broken down by category for any time period
• Multi-currency — log expenses in any currency with automatic conversion to your home currency
• Home screen widget — your daily total always one glance away
• Biometric lock — Face ID / Touch ID to keep your finances private
• iCloud-free — SwiftData storage, stays on your device

No ads. No tracking. No account required.
